Tomato Soup

Description

That grilled cheese is gonna need a partner

Ingredients

  • Campbells Tomato Soup
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pour soup out of the can and into a pot
  2. Fill the now empty can with milk and add it to the pot, stir up the milk in the can first to clean the rest of the soup out
  3. Turn the burner on a low heat and let the soup warm up. Stir to avoid burning and don't let it boil
