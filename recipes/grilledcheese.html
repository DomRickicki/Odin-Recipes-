Grilled Cheese

Description

Some of that excellent grilled cheese for tomato soup dipping when you're not feeling so well.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Gouda
  • Provolone
  • Brie
  • onions
  • butter
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. Grill Onions

    Carmelize those bad boys in butter while you prep the rest

  2. Butter yo bread

    Mayo first! both sides get mayo, then butter

  3. Apply cheese

    Provolone first, some thin slices of gouda and then some thin slices of brie on top

  4. Add Onions

    add the onions on top of the cheese

  5. Grill!

    put it on the pan and grill until golden brown on both sides. use a lid to help melt cheese in the middle of the sandwich
